About

Hi, I'm Lisa.

I started Ready Jet Roam to help women travel smarter and safer — without the fear, the overwhelm, or the sense that you're supposed to already know all this. Everything here comes from the road, not a brochure.

Join the newsletter → See the guides

What began as a travel blog in 2018 has become something I care about a lot more: a place that helps people — especially women travelling solo — feel genuinely prepared instead of anxious.

These days that lives across a few places: a weekly newsletter, a growing shelf of guides and books, a packing app, and this site tying it together. The look and voice are the same everywhere on purpose — so wherever you meet Ready Jet Roam, it feels like the same person talking.

You don't have to travel fearlessly. You just have to travel prepared.

If that's the kind of travelling you want to do, the newsletter is the best place to start — it's the one thing I put the most into every week.
